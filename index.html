PVBiz is a solar monitoring solution that provides near real-time insights and smart notifications for Sunsynk, Solarman, Deye, Sol-Ark, Solar Assistant and LuxPower plants. It helps you optimize your solar system's performance and stay informed about its health.

To get started, simply sign up for a free trial on our platform. Once registered, you can connect your solar plants and start monitoring your system in real-time.

You need to share your Solar Plant to allow PVBiz to access your data(Only read only visitor access required). For Sunsynk, please share your plant with PVBiz, by sharing the plant to this email - admin@pvbiz.co.za. For Solarman or Deye, under your Authorizations, search for PVBiz under businesses, and share the plant as a visitor.

PVBiz provides real-time notifications via Email, Telegram, and Push notifications through browser and mobile app. You can customize rules for 10 inverter metrics. Note: WhatsApp notifications are no longer included in regular subscriptions due to pricing changes, but custom pricing options are available.
